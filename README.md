# 🔐 Phishing Email Detection & Awareness

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing phishing emails and creating awareness about how such attacks work. The goal is to identify phishing indicators, classify risks, and provide preventive guidelines for users.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze real-world email samples
* Identify phishing characteristics
* Classify email risk levels
* Educate users on avoiding phishing attacks

---

## 📧 Email Analysis

### 1️⃣ Internship Scam Email

* Suspicious domain impersonating a trusted company
* Use of urgency and generic greeting
* Google Form used for data collection
* **Risk Level:** ⚠️ Suspicious

---

### 2️⃣ Aptitude Platform Email

* Promotional email from Gmail
* Secure link but lacks verification
* No spoofing detected
* **Risk Level:** ⚠️ Suspicious / Promotional

---

### 3️⃣ Placement Program Scam

* Use of social proof and urgency
* Third-party form link
* Non-corporate mail infrastructure
* **Risk Level:** ❌ Phishing

---

## 🧠 Key Phishing Indicators

* Fake or unverified sender domains
* Urgent or threatening language
* Suspicious links
* Generic greetings
* Requests for personal information

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Google Message Header Analyzer
* MXToolbox
* Web Browser (link inspection)

---

## 🛡️ Prevention Tips

### ✔ Do’s

* Verify sender email addresses
* Hover over links before clicking
* Use official websites
* Report suspicious emails

### ❌ Don’ts

* Do not click unknown links
* Do not share passwords or OTPs
* Do not trust urgent messages blindly

---

## 📊 Outcome

This project helped in understanding phishing techniques and how to detect malicious or suspicious emails effectively. It also highlights the importance of cybersecurity awareness.
